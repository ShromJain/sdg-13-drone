About the drone

It is a low cost, easily operable prototype designed to spray chemicals onto stubble that can decompose it quickly, de-incentivising farmers from burning it and preventing air pollution and smog alongside the release of CO2, and producing manure that is helpful for farmers. It can also monitor crop growth and nutrient deficiencies, thus increasing yields which lead to less land clearance and deforestation for agriculture, thereby helping alleviate the climate crisis. Thus it addresses climate action directly and indirectly. .

About Us

Our team’s plan is to not just focus on one aspect of the issue, but to combine both. We plan to thus showcase DPS’ efforts towards SDG-13 climate action.
